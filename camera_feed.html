3D Scanning Help

  • Use a neutral colored background that contrasts with your object
  • Avoid reflective or transparent surfaces when possible
  • For small objects, position the camera 30-50cm away
  • Rotate the object in small increments (15-20°) between captures
  • Maintain consistent lighting throughout all captures

Blurry images: Check camera focus and ensure the object isn't moving during capture.

Poor reconstruction: Try capturing more angles (minimum 8 recommended).

Camera not connecting: Verify your camera is properly connected and powered on.

Dark images: Adjust lighting or increase exposure in camera settings.
